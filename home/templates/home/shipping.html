Shipping & Returns

Standard delivery charges on all orders under 40 Euro. Delivery will take between 2-3 days. Please note that a signature may be required upon receipt of your delivery with proof of age being over 21.

You can only return your order if the Get A Grape bottles are unopened and sealed. Due to health & safety reasons, we cannot accept any goods that have been opened. If you wish to return the bottle(s), you can notify us within 14 days of receiving your order that you have changed your mind. Please keep in mind you will have to cover the return shipping cost. Please use the contact form to inform us of the returns. You need to return the bottle(s) to us within 14 days of notifying us. If the goods are re-sellable we will reimburse your order. If your product is faulty or damaged, you can ask for an exchange or a refund within 30 days of receiving your order and we will pay for return postage.

Please inform us by email of the reason for the returns and we will handle from there. If any further information is required we will be in contact. Please take in to consideration the below requirements when returning products.

1. Please send your returns with a reputable company and make sure the product is well packaged.
2. If possible please include a picture of the product with or without the defect.

We work quickly to pick and pack your items. If possible we will endeavour to cancel your order or change it. If tghe order has been dispatched you can always return the order to us.

Please use our contact page if you require any other queries to be answered.
